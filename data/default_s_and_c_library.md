### S&C Routine Definitions

**General Guidelines for all S&C:**
*   **Warm-up:** 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., jogging, cycling) and dynamic stretches (leg swings, arm circles).
*   **Cool-down:** 5-10 minutes of static stretches, holding each stretch for 20-30 seconds.
*   **Progression:** As you get stronger, you can increase reps, sets, or introduce light weights/resistance bands.
*   **Rest:** 60-90 seconds between sets.

---

#### S&C Routine 1: Core Focus (Approx. 35 mins)

This routine targets core stability and strength, crucial for running efficiency and injury prevention.

1.  **Plank:** 3 sets, hold for 45-60 seconds (or as long as form is maintained).
    *   *Focus:* Keep a straight line from head to heels, engage glutes and abs.
2.  **Side Plank:** 3 sets per side, hold for 30-45 seconds.
    *   *Focus:* Stack hips, keep body in a straight line, engage obliques.
3.  **Bird-Dog:** 3 sets, 10-12 reps per side.
    *   *Focus:* Move slowly and controlled, keeping core stable, avoid arching the lower back.
4.  **Dead Bug:** 3 sets, 10-12 reps per side.
    *   *Focus:* Keep lower back pressed into the floor, move opposing arm and leg slowly.
5.  **Glute Bridge:** 3 sets, 12-15 reps.
    *   *Focus:* Squeeze glutes at the top, lift hips until body forms a straight line from shoulders to knees.
6.  **Hollow Body Hold (Advanced):** 3 sets, hold for 20-30 seconds (or as long as form is maintained).
    *   *Focus:* Lower back pressed into floor, arms and legs extended, constant abdominal tension. Start with bent knees if too difficult.

---

#### S&C Routine 2: Lower Body Focus (Approx. 35 mins)

This routine builds leg strength, power, and muscular endurance.

1.  **Bodyweight Squats:** 3 sets, 12-15 reps.
    *   *Focus:* Chest up, back straight, squat as if sitting in a chair, knees tracking over toes.
2.  **Lunges (Alternating):** 3 sets, 10-12 reps per leg.
    *   *Focus:* Step forward, lower back knee towards the ground (hovering), keep front knee behind toes. Maintain balance.
3.  **Calf Raises (Standing):** 3 sets, 15-20 reps (bilateral or single-leg).
    *   *Focus:* Full range of motion, controlled movement up and down.
4.  **Single-Leg Deadlift (Bodyweight or light weight):** 3 sets, 8-10 reps per leg.
    *   *Focus:* Hinge at the hip, keep back straight, extend non-standing leg behind you for balance.
5.  **Step-Ups (onto a sturdy bench/box):** 3 sets, 10-12 reps per leg.
    *   *Focus:* Drive through the heel of the leading foot, stand tall at the top, control the descent.
6.  **Wall Sit:** 3 sets, hold for 45-60 seconds.
    *   *Focus:* Back flat against wall, thighs parallel to the floor, 90-degree bend at knees.

---

#### S&C Routine 3: Upper Body & Back Focus (Approx. 35 mins)

This routine supports posture, arm drive, and overall running economy.

1.  **Push-ups:** 3 sets, as many reps as possible with good form (AMRAP).
    *   *Focus:* Keep body in a straight line, chest to elbow height. Modify on knees or against an incline if needed.
2.  **Dumbbell Rows (or resistance band rows):** 3 sets, 10-12 reps per arm.
    *   *Focus:* Maintain a flat back, pull elbow towards ceiling, squeeze shoulder blade.
3.  **Overhead Press (light dumbbells or bodyweight):** 3 sets, 10-12 reps.
    *   *Focus:* Press weights straight overhead, control the eccentric phase. If using bodyweight, consider pike push-ups.
4.  **Bicep Curls (light dumbbells or resistance band):** 3 sets, 12-15 reps.
    *   *Focus:* Keep elbows tucked, controlled curl, avoid swinging.
5.  **Tricep Extensions (overhead or kickbacks, light dumbbells):** 3 sets, 12-15 reps.
    *   *Focus:* Isolate the triceps, controlled movement.
6.  **Supermans:** 3 sets, 12-15 reps.
    *   *Focus:* Lift arms, chest, and legs simultaneously, engaging lower back and glutes. Controlled movement.

#### S&C Routine 4: Full Body Circuit (Approx. 35 mins)

This circuit combines elements for comprehensive conditioning and mobility. Perform each exercise for 45 seconds, then rest for 15 seconds before moving to the next. Complete 3 rounds with 1-2 minutes rest between rounds.

1.  **Jumping Jacks (or Marching in Place for low impact)**
    *   *Focus:* Light cardio to elevate heart rate and warm up muscles.
2.  **Squats (Bodyweight)**
    *   *Focus:* See Routine 2.
3.  **Push-ups (on knees or toes)**
    *   *Focus:* See Routine 3.
4.  **Mountain Climbers:**
    *   *Focus:* Maintain plank position, drive knees to chest alternately, controlled pace.
5.  **Alternating Lunges**
    *   *Focus:* See Routine 2.
6.  **Russian Twists (bodyweight or light weight):**
    *   *Focus:* Sit with knees bent, lean back slightly, twist torso side to side, engaging obliques.
7.  **Bird-Dog**
    *   *Focus:* See Routine 1.
8.  **Plank**
    *   *Focus:* See Routine 1.
