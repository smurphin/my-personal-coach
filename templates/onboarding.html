Use Private Notes for Context

The more context, the better the coaching

Add details in Strava's private notes about how the session felt. This helps the AI understand what's working and adjust your plan.

Track Injuries & Recovery

Consistent tracking = better injury management

The AI tracks pain levels and progress over time. This helps identify patterns and adjust training to prevent aggravation.

Example progression:

Week 1: "Knee pain 6/10 after intervals"

Week 2: "Knee pain 4/10 on easy runs"

Week 3: "Knee feels fine, pain 1/10"

Important: The AI can suggest reduced load, different exercises, or when to consult a professional based on your notes.
